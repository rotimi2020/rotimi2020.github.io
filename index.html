About Me

I'm an Operations Analyst & Data Analyst with a B.Sc. in Computer Science (Hons, 2019) and an IBM Data Science Professional Certificate. I specialise in Operations Analytics — building robust, end‑to‑end data workflows that transform raw operational data into clear insights, interactive dashboards, and deployed web applications.
