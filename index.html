About Me

I'm a Data Analyst with a B.Sc. in Computer Science (Hons, 2019) and an IBM Data Science Professional Certificate. I specialise in Operations Analytics — building robust, end‑to‑end data workflows that transform raw operational data into clear insights, interactive dashboards, and production‑ready web applications.

My portfolio demonstrates a complete analytics lifecycle using real construction operations data, from raw WhatsApp logs → Excel cleaning → SQL modelling → Python analysis → Power BI dashboards → Django web deployment. This framework is industry‑agnostic and equally applicable to healthcare, manufacturing, logistics, energy, and finance.

What I deliver:

  • ETL & Data Pipelines: Python (Pandas, NumPy), SQL, Excel (Power Query)
  • Dashboards & BI: Power BI, Django, Streamlit, Plotly
  • Predictive Analytics: Scikit‑Learn, time‑series forecasting, classification
  • Business Impact: ₦4.5M+ reconciled, 95.7% utilisation, 1,948m excavation analysed

I'm actively seeking Data Analyst or Operations Analyst roles where I can apply analytics, automation, and business intelligence to improve operational performance and drive data‑driven decision‑making.
